My 3D learning experience with Blender

From 2D to 3D

For my next game, I wanted to experiment with 3d models. After I found the technic to render 3d models to 2d sprite. I believe that could help me with the creation of assets.

My first attempt

After following the Blender tutorial from Blender Guru, I create my donut animation. This image below was rendered with the cycles engine. However, the video was made with Eevee (waiting on the AMD driver for Linux). I also almost finished making my cookie by myself in the same style as the donut.

Final Thought

While not being an artist and being intimated by the complexity of Blender, I quickly found my mark. I am not assuming to be an expert, but I can try to design a simple model for my game. I found the process fun and addicting.
